About Football


What is Football?


Football(Soccer If you're American) is a sport

played by two teams of 11. Theese teams try to

score in the other team's goal (wich is defened

by the goal keeper)


Where does it come from?


Football Comes from England in the 12th century.

In this century, games that resembled football

were played on meadows and roads in England.
